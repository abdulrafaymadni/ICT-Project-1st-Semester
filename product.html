A masterpiece of English tailoring, The Westminster Three-Piece Suit represents the pinnacle of our craft. Cut from premium Super 150s Merino wool sourced from the finest Italian mills, this suit features a subtle midnight navy with an understated pinstripe that catches the light with refined elegance.

Each suit is constructed using traditional Savile Row techniques, including hand-stitched lapels, working surgeon's cuffs, and a half-canvas chest piece for superior drape and longevity. The waistcoat features mother-of-pearl buttons and an adjustable back strap for the perfect fit.
